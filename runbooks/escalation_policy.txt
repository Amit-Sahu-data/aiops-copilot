ESCALATION POLICY

SEVERITY LEVELS:
- SEV1: Full outage or data loss risk. Page on-call immediately, no automated remediation without
  explicit human approval at every step.
- SEV2: Degraded service (latency, partial errors, repeated restarts). Automated investigation and
  remediation proposals are allowed, but all write actions require human approval before execution.
- SEV3: Minor or isolated issue with no immediate user impact. Investigate and document; remediation
  can wait for normal business hours review.

GENERAL RULE:
No automated system in this environment is permitted to execute a write action (pod restart,
resource limit change, scaling) without explicit human approval, regardless of severity. This
applies even when the root cause is well-established and the fix seems obvious.
